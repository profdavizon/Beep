🚀 Quick Start Guide
1️⃣
Set Up Your Business
Go to Admin → Business Settings and enter your business name, KRA PIN, M-Pesa Paybill, and contact details.
2️⃣
Add Your Products
Go to Inventory and add all your products with cost price, sell price, category, and barcode. Use the 📷 scan button to scan barcodes directly.
3️⃣
Add Staff
Go to Admin → Staff Credentials to create staff accounts. Assign roles: Bar Staff, Manager, Admin, or Owner.
4️⃣
Start Selling
Go to POS, tap items to add to the order, select payment method, and complete the sale. Stock updates automatically.
5️⃣
Track & Report
Use Reports for daily Z-reports, sales summaries, and staff performance. Export to Excel for your accountant.
6️⃣
Back Up Your Data
Go to Settings → Google Drive Backup to save your data to the cloud, or export to Excel from any report screen.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
▶ What happens if I clear my browser cache?
All data is stored in localStorage. Clearing the cache or browser data will erase it. Always back up to Google Drive or export to Excel regularly to prevent data loss.
▶ Can multiple devices use the same data?
Yes — when connected to the internet, data syncs automatically in real time via Firebase across all logged-in devices. The Live badge in the topbar confirms sync is active. Offline changes are stored locally and pushed when connection resumes. Use Settings → Google Drive Backup for an extra offline copy.
▶ How do I reset a staff member's password?
Go to Admin → Staff Credentials Register. Find the staff member and use the reset password option. You can also send the new credentials via Gmail directly from that screen.
▶ How does the barcode scanner work?
Tap the 📷 button on the POS search bar, product form, or stock-in modal to open the scanner. Camera mode uses your phone/laptop camera. USB/Bluetooth scanners work automatically — just connect and scan. Manual mode lets you type or paste a barcode.
▶ What is a Z-Report?
A Z-Report (End-of-Day Report) is a daily financial summary showing total sales, payments by method, expenses, and net cash position. Generated from Reports → Summary Reports → Z-Report. Required for KRA compliance.
▶ Is my data safe and private?
Yes. Data is stored in your own Firebase project (which you control) and cached locally in the browser. Firebase uses Google's security infrastructure. Staff passwords are excluded from all backups and exports. The app works fully offline and syncs when reconnected.
